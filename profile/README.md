### 🚀 The Mission

Today, massive Web3 lending protocols want to fund real estate developers, but they are completely blind to the physical world. Verifying a property's zoning status requires human analysts manually reading 500-page municipal PDFs—a bottleneck that costs thousands of dollars, takes days to complete, and completely breaks the speed of DeFi. 

**Townhall eliminates this friction.** By leveraging decentralized oracle networks and high-speed settlement layers, we turn a multi-day human paper-chase into a 3-second smart contract execution.

---

### 🏗️ What We Build

Townhall operates as a dual-sided ecosystem to solve both data friction for lenders and capital friction for developers.

**1. The Verification Hub (For Lenders)**
* A seamless portal where DeFi lending protocols can input a Parcel ID and instantly receive a cryptographically verified "Rezoning Score." 
* Crawling agents autonomously scrape local legislative websites, and decentralized nodes reach consensus on the entitlements, setbacks, and zoning rules before pushing the truth on-chain.

**2. The Tokenization Portal (For Developers)**
* A frictionless platform allowing mid-market land developers to bypass institutional gatekeepers.
* Once a parcel's deed and KYC are verified off-chain, our smart contracts automatically mint fractional shares of the real estate, allowing developers to raise capital directly from the public.

---

### ⚙️ Core Architecture & Tech Stack

Our infrastructure relies on enterprise-grade Web3 protocols to ensure lenders can trust the data and developers can scale their fractional assets.

* **Data Ingestion:** Python & FastAPI (Building highly resilient crawling agents to parse unstructured government JSON and PDF responses).
* **The Oracle Layer:** Chainlink Custom Runtime Environment (CRE) (Running custom compute logic across decentralized nodes to reach consensus on the Rezoning Score).
* **Decentralized Storage:** Walrus (Anchoring heavy, 5MB+ city council PDFs and zoning maps securely without bloating the smart contract).
* **The Settlement Layer:** Hedera Blockchain (Utilizing EVM compatibility and the Hedera Token Service (HTS) to mint ERC-1155 fractional shares with predictable, micro-cent fees).
